I'm glad you found me!

Hello, My name is Kaitlyn Gray. I’m currently in my final year of the Graphic Design program at Wake Technical Community College, where I’m sharpening my skills and preparing to turn my passion into a career. Alongside my studies, I work as a part-time photographer and social media coordinator, and I’m also a proud dog mom! My journey into graphic design and photography began back in 2016 during my high school yearbook class, where I first picked up a DSLR camera and experimented with creative page layouts. That spark turned into a passion, leading me to buy my own camera in 2018 and dive deeper into photography. Now, as I work toward my degree, I’m excited to blend my love for design and photography to create meaningful visual experiences. Though I'm just starting my professional journey, I'm excited to bring new perspectives, creativity, and enthusiasm to every design.
